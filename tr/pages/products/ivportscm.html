Ivport Stereo Compute Module is breakout board that you can plug a Raspberry Pi Compute Modules into. It comes with two Camera ports, 10/100M Ethernet, dual USB 2.0 ports, Classic Raspberry Pi GPIO header, HDMI output, microSD slot and PoE (Power over Ethernet) support. It is also compatible with sorts of Raspberry Pi HATs.

ÖZELLİKLER

  • Camera Input:

    • 2 × 15-pin Raspberry Pi Camera

  • Video

    • 1 × full size HDMI

  • GPIO

    • Classic Raspberry Pi 40-pin GPIO header For connecting sorts of Raspberry Pi HATs

  • Network

    • 10/100 BaseT Ethernet socket

  • USB

    • 2 x USB 2.0 Connector

  • SD card

    • MicroSD
