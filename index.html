ABOUT US

Ivmech Mechatronics and Innovation was founded in 2012 by a young, experienced, dynamic, and well-educated team, with the aim of providing innovative and creative solutions for industrial and civilian applications by combining various axis control applications with advanced electromechanical systems.

In addition, our company provides project services for various engineering disciplines and, within the field of mechatronics, specializes in the development of all kinds of mechanical, electronic, software-based products, as well as the design, manufacturing, and prototyping of systems tailored to specific requirements.
