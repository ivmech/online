TWO WHEELED SELF BALANCING VEHICLE (ITAR)

ITAR is a self-balancing electric personal transportation device.

ITAR features an axial inverted pendulum servo motion system comprised of inertial sensors and direct current motors. This system employs a dynamic gyroscopic control system to maintain balance of the driver on it by increasing and decreasing the rotation speeds of the motors, enabling lateral movements along the horizontal axis.

Using ITAR is as easy as walking. It is designed for effortless use both indoors and outdoors.

FEATURES

  • Electronic key for opening
  • Steering sensitivity proportional to speed
  • Speed, battery, and temperature status indicator
  • Remote controllable
  • Long-distance travel capability
  • Obstacle detection and collision prevention
  • Driver detection
  • Lightweight and durable body
  • Carbon fiber fender
  • Affordable price

LIMITS

  • Maximum load capacity: 90 kg
  • Maximum speed: 18 km/h (Software-imposed speed limit)
  • Battery: 33.3 V Li-Polymer 200Wh
  • Range: 10 km
  • Usage time: 8 hours
  • Center of gravity: As close as possible to the driver's center of gravity
  • ITAR has the capability to detect hazardous situations. Despite all safety precautions, as it is a system operated under driver control, it may not provide complete protection to the driver.

DIMENSIONS

  • Height: 1250 mm – 1650 mm
  • Platform Height: 250 mm
  • Width: 600 mm
  • Wheel Diameter: 410 mm
  • Weight: 35 kg
