ROBOT ARM

Educational Aesthetic Robotic Arm

It is a modular and aesthetically designed robotic arm with 6 degrees of freedom that can be easily used at the laboratory level for educational, research, and development purposes.

CAPABLE

  • 6 Degrees of Freedom

    • The arm can move with 6 degrees of freedom.

  • 1,25kg Payload

    • In the middle position, the maximum load is 1.25kg. When the arm is fully extended, the maximum load is 750grams. This is sufficient for everyday objects.

  • 70cm Reach

    • The arm can reach a maximum of 70cm.

  • Control

    • It can be used with position or speed control in Cartesian or Angular modes.

  • Maximum Speed

    • It can move at a speed of up to 20 cm/s.

STRONG

  • Lightness

    • Its total weight is only 5 kg. It can be easily used in portable applications.

  • Unique Design

    • It has a unique design with a modular and aesthetic structure.

  • It is weather-resistant.

    • It has water resistance in accordance with IPX2 standards.

  • Quiet

    • It can move almost silently, smoothly, and with great precision thanks to the use of high-quality materials and precise assembly.

  • 2 Fingers Movable Gripper

    • At the fingertips, there are flexible fillings to increase the contact surface.

FLEXIBLE

  • Comprehensive Sensor Package

    • There are sensors in each joint that can measure torque, position, motor current, temperature, and acceleration, and these sensors can be monitored through an API.

  • API

    • It is compatible with Windows and Linux operating systems via USB 2.0. It can be programmed using C++, C#, or ROS (Robot Operating System) through an API.

  • Low Power Consumption

    • It can operate with a 24V battery or a standard power outlet. It consumes energy equivalent to that of a light bulb.

APPLICATION AREAS

  • Research and Development
  • Education and Laboratory
  • Rehabilitation, Assistance, and Medical
  • Remote Control Systems
