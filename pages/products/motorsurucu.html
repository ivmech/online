MOTOR DRIVERS

Drivers for Brushed DC, Brushless DC, and AC Servo Motors

Efficient motor drivers capable of high-power speed or torque control, suitable for different types of motors in various applications.

IVMS-80A Brushed DC Motor Driver

The IVMS-80A is designed as a brushed DC motor driver for applications requiring motor control, allowing for high-current brushed DC motors' speed and direction control.

  • Speed control with Pulse Width Modulation (PWM)
  • Simple and user-friendly
  • Wide input voltage range
  • Wide control voltage range
  • Domestic production and technical support
